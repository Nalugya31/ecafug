Clean Drinking Water

In this situation, 73% of the people in our community face in daily life. We have no boreholes for free water where people make long lines just to fetch 1 or 2 Jerry cans of water from a pond in a forest. This has led to poor sanitation and bad personal hygiene.

Young children of between 3-15 years collide at dirty ponds of water just not to move so far which easily spreads diseases like cholera, dysentery, and athletes' foot. The ponds are close to 3 at a very big distance from one another. Children have to move for close to 3 km just to reach the ponds or stream to fetch water. Many people in the society have resorted to the use of dirty or no water just for the sake of avoiding moving long distances.

What hurts most is that people with bicycles and motorcycles have resorted to selling water. They go fetch much water at the boreholes and then start moving selling water to people at a very high price like a 20-liter Jerry can is bought for close to $1-2. Let's join hands together and fundraise for the #Good_cause and make the world a better place. I request you to please join us to construct more boreholes and extend water even closer to the people so as to reduce the risks brought about by the lack of water in the community.

Save Pregnant mothers

Supporting pregnant youth is crucial for ensuring their well-being and that of their unborn children. These young mothers face unique challenges as they navigate pregnancy and early parenthood. By offering comprehensive support services, including medical care, education, and emotional guidance, we can empower them to make informed choices and provide a nurturing environment for their children. Your support can make a meaningful difference in the lives of these young families, offering them hope for a brighter future. Let's come together to ensure that pregnant youth receive the care and support they need to thrive.
