About Us

Welcome to Exodus Community Awakening Organization Uganda

ECAOUG is a nonprofit dedicated to serving orphaned, elderly, vulnerable communities, and vulnerable kids. Our story began with a simple yet powerful vision: to empower the vulnerable and provide them with the support and resources they need to thrive.

From humble beginnings to a brighter future, our story is a testament to the power of compassion and resilience. Join us as we continue to write this story, one life at a time.

Founded in 2018

Work Since 2018 with Pride!!

Our nonprofit organization was founded in 2018 with a mission to empower orphaned, elderly, vulnerable communities, and vulnerable kids. Since then, we have been dedicated to providing support, resources, and hope to those who need it most. ok

Our Mission

Mission is to make everyone smile.

We believe that every individual deserves dignity, love, and care. Our mission is to serve, support, and empower vulnerable communities, providing them with the essentials for a brighter future.

Our Values

Compassion, empathy, and service are at the heart of everything we do. We believe in the power of community and partnership, and we invite you to join us on this journey of transformation and hope.

Our Team
Our team is comprised of dedicated individuals united by a common cause. We are passionate about serving vulnerable communities and making a difference in their lives.
